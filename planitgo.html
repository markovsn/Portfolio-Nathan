PLANIT GO

Mobile application

Challenge

Design a mobile application that solves a user problem associated with the planning of travel.

Solution

PlanIt Go simplifies the travel planning experience by helping users feel familiar with unfamiliar destinations and plan travel in a fun, flexible and collaborative way.

Client

UI/UX Bootcamp - Individual project

My role

Sole UI/UX Designer

Timing

Three weeks

Research

User Insights

I conducted seven user interviews with potential users between the ages of 23 and 55 years.

I found that young people, between ages 20 and 30, may struggle to plan travel with friends due to conflicting interests and budgets.

They tend to use multiple apps and websites, which results in information being lost or duplicated. They are also more likely to book through a travel agent if planning feels too complicated or unfamiliar.

Persona

I developed a persona to better understand the goals and frustrations of a typical user, and help identify solutions.

How might we improve the travel planning process so that young travellers are motivated and inspired to book their next holiday together?

Ideation

Value Proposition

I prepared a value proposition matrix following brainstorming of potential feature ideas. The core features that I prioritised were: 

  • Live and interactive planning tools that users can invite fellow travellers to.
  • Itinerary creator that can extract information from booking confirmations.
  • A library of location-specific information and recommendations to facilitate planning.

    • Competitor Analysis

    A high-level analysis of competitor products found that they:

  • Lack collaboration and communication tools to help users to plan travel with company.
  • Do not allow users to create and publicly share itineraries or display other users’ itineraries for inspiration.
  • Lack budgeting tools to help users track and split expenses.

    • Storyboard

    I designed a storyboard to visually showcase the journey a typical user would take once downloading PlanIt Go. It helped me imagine how to design the key features before sketching.

    Designing

    Sketching

    I used quick sketch iterations to visualise various layouts before wireframing.

    Wireframing

    I focused on wireframing the high impact pages to demonstrate functionality and conduct user testing.

    Testing

    I conducted five user tests to understand how effectively users could navigate through the app and complete core tasks. A number of pain points were identified and addressed in the proceeding version.
